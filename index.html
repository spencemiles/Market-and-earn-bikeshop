Understanding Your Target Market

Learn where to find your ideal customers and how to identify potential buyers

Where Are the Customers?

  • Cycling clubs
  • Fitness groups and gyms
  • University/college students
  • Young professionals
  • Delivery riders
  • Outdoor enthusiasts

Spotting Potential Buyers

  • Likes or shares bike-related posts
  • Comments on outdoor or fitness content
  • Follows cycling or gym accounts
  • Responds positively to your posts
  • Engages with fitness communities
  • Seeks affordable transport solutions

Effective Conversion Strategies

  • Offer limited-time group discounts
  • Create referral incentive programs
  • Share user-generated content/testimonials
  • Highlight cost vs. public transport savings
  • Demonstrate eco-friendly benefits
  • Provide flexible payment options
How to Engage with Cycling Clubs

Partner with local cycling clubs for demo days and group rides. Offer club discounts and sponsor events to build relationships with passionate cyclists who can become brand advocates.

Targeting University Students

Set up campus booths during orientation weeks. Emphasize the cost-saving benefits compared to public transport. Offer student discounts and flexible payment plans to make bikes more accessible.

Reaching Delivery Riders

Focus on durability and low maintenance costs. Highlight how our bikes are designed for daily commercial use with reinforced frames and all-weather components. Offer special commercial rider packages.

Perfect Picture Tips

1. Use natural lighting (morning or evening)

2. Stand confidently beside the bike

3. Smile and show excitement

4. Wear sporty or casual clothes

Captions That Convert
