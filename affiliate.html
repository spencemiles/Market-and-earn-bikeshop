Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly via your chosen payment method (PayPal or bank transfer). Payments are processed around the 15th of each month for the previous month's earnings, provided you've reached the minimum $50 threshold.

How long does the approval process take?

We typically review applications within 2 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your application is approved or if we need any additional information.

Can I promote on social media?

Absolutely! Many of our top affiliates promote through Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and other social platforms. Just be sure to comply with each platform's terms of service regarding affiliate links.

Is there a cost to join?

No, joining our affiliate program is completely free. There are no hidden fees or charges.

How are returns handled?

If a customer returns a product they purchased through your link, the commission for that sale will be deducted from your next payment. This ensures fair compensation for valid sales only.
