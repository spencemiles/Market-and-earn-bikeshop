Product Description

The TrailMaster Pro mountain bike is engineered for performance on the most challenging trails. Its lightweight yet durable aluminum frame provides the perfect balance of strength and agility, while the advanced suspension system absorbs impacts for a smooth ride.

Key features include:

  • Lightweight aluminum frame with hydroformed tubing
  • 120mm travel front suspension fork with lockout
  • Shimano Deore 1x11-speed drivetrain for reliable shifting
  • Tektro hydraulic disc brakes for confident stopping power
  • 27.5" tubeless-ready wheels with aggressive tread tires
  • Dropper post compatible for technical descents

Technical Specifications

Frame 6061 Aluminum, 120mm travel
Fork SR Suntour XCR 34, 120mm travel
Drivetrain Shimano Deore 1x11-speed
Brakes Tektro HD-M275 hydraulic disc, 180mm rotors
Wheels 27.5" alloy, tubeless-ready
Tires Maxxis Ardent, 27.5x2.4"
Weight 13.2 kg (29.1 lbs)
Sizes S, M, L, XL
Recommended Rider Height S: 5'1"-5'5", M: 5'5"-5'9", L: 5'9"-6'1", XL: 6'1"-6'5"
